Dungeons and Dust Bunnies

This is my Codeup capstone project. A fully functional task management website was created by my team and I. The website uses the user's to-do list to create a battle against a monster, and when tasks are completed and crossed off, the monster takes damage until it is defeated. The website records battles and issues badges for both the quantity of battles and for every unique monster faced.
